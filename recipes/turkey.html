Curry Ground Turkey With Rice & Peas

Curry ground turkey with rice and peas is full of warm, rich flavors but is super quick and easy to make!

Back to Odin Recipes

Prep Time: Cook Time: Total Time:
5 min 15 min 20 min

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 small onion, diced small
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lb. lean ground turkey (I use 93% lean)
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder or garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • ¾ teaspoon kosher salt
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric
  • ½ teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 (14.5 oz.) can diced tomatoes (undrained)
  • 1 cup frozen peas

Instructions

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add olive oil.
  2. Add onion and sauté for 4-5 minutes, until softened. Add garlic and cook another minute.
  3. Add ground turkey the pan and and cook until browned through, about 7-8 minutes.
  4. Add the curry, cumin, coriander, salt, turmeric and ginger and stir well to combine.
  5. Stir in the tomatoes and peas until warmed through, 2-3 minutes.
  6. Serve the curried turkey and peas over steamed rice. Sprinkle with fresh parsley or cilantro and enjoy!
